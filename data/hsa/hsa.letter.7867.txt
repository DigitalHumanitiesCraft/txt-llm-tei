[Postkarte LONDON 7 JU 82]
 London, 64 StGeorge Square SW June 10/82 
Ich danken Sich for your letter of May 29: - I send you a Manuscript copy of the paper in the Singapúr branch of the Asiatic Society = send it back when you have copied it: I will try and get copies of Scribners Monthly – and New Quartets – I have seen the latter – Yokohaio English (note 1) – a good paper – I shall be glad next December to insert a paper of yours in the Journal of the Royal Asiatic – My Book is on the „Languages of Africa“ (note 2) all that is known about them: thank you for mentioning the Grammar of Mbunda or Angela by Souza &amp; Alves (note 3)
I have written to Leiton for it
Robert Cust
