I send herewith my ‘History of Language’ (note 1) in which you will be able to see the influence of some of your own views. But I do not know what yr opinion is about the affinity of Aryan & Finnish. (note 2) I should be glad to know whether you approve of Gabelentz’s affiliation of Bask to Berber. (note 3) I have some difficulty in believg it, & yet, as far as I can judge without knowing the languages, his comparisons are sound.

H. Sweet
