BRITISH MUSEUM
 LONDON : W. C.
 19.2.92.

Dear Dr 
Rost, (note 1)
I have not received any official note yet, but perhaps it will come in a day or two.
I asked Mr Thompson about Prince Lucien Bonaparte’s (note 2) books, but he does not know of any having come yet. I tried to see Dr Garnett, (note 3) but failed; when I see him I will ask him.
Many thanks for arranging for the prolonged loan of the MS.
I am, yrs very truly,

EA Wallis Budge (note 4)
