Baltimore, 3/17, 1887.
My dear Sir,
Accept best thanks, I pray you, for the Separatabdruck of the Literaturblatt (note 1) containing your valuable and interesting review of ‚O Crêolo de Cabo Verde‘. (note 2) I shall take pleasure in calling the attention of the readers of ‘Mod. Lang. Notes‘ (note 3) to it.
Hoping that you are very well,

I am yours sincerely,

A.M.Elliott
