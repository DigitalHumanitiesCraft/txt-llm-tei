[POSTCARD, LONDON JY 17 82]
 64, St. George’s Square,
 15/7/82 S. W.
Many thanks for your letter with notes of African Creole Dialects, (note 1) and your Postcard since received – I think that the information which you have kindly sent will meet all my requirements. I hope to hear from you after your return from your holiday. (note 2) I shall go to Algiers and Tunis in october –
Robert Cust

_____________________________________________1 Schuchardt, „Kreolische Studien I. Ueber das Negerportugiesische von S. Thomé (Westafrika)“, Sitzungsberichte der philosophisch-historischen Classe der Kaiserlichen Akademie der Wissenschaften. Wien 101, 1882, 889-917.2 Alexanderbad.
