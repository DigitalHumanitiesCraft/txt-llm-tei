April 17. 1871 (note 1)
My dear Schuchardt,
I am just going to start from here (London) tomorrow for Göttingen: for it is to Göttingen that I have made up my mind to go. Would you kindly send off my books for Göttingen as soon as you conveniently can. I have with me 2 copies of the Barδ Cwsg (note 2) and two Welsh Bibles Would you like to have one of each? Professor Leskien (note 3) wishes to have a bible. I can give you no address now of course; poste restante of course will do I should think.
Yours very truly,
J. Rhŷs
