[POST CARD---GREAT BRITAN &amp; IRELAND; LONDON S.W. MY 12 84] 
Yours of April 26. I know nothing of „Fox or Cox Ensanos or Ensinos“ (Burnell is dead) (note 1) – nor do I know anything of F Marryatt, „Guar“: (note 2) - The Report is of the Moslem, or Mahometan Mission to the Malays+ in the Cape of Good Hope: we have very little information about them. Dr Büttner (note 3) of the Rheinish Mission of Barmen is now in Germany: no new books have appeared lately
RNC
 64 St George Square SW
 10/5/84

+ (von anderer Hand / Schuchardt (?): „bezieht sich auf Dr Fox, Malay Patois / Report of the Moslem Mission“) (note 4)
