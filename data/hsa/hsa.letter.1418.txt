My dear Prof. Schuchardt,
The reprints of the Litteraturblat [sic] (note 1) with your notice of Baissac etc. (note 2) have just been received. Accept best thanks for them. I have had many letters from different parts of the united states [sic], thanking me for calling special attention to your book. (note 3) I trust it may have a larger sale in this country where we so badly need correct [sic] philological views disseminated. I have no doubt you have done a good missionary labor, though unconsciously, in this far off country by  this important publication. I shall be out with my second article on Canadian French (note 4) in the course of next month. I hope to get through, in this, with the preliminary questions that touch that general subject.
Hoping you are very well,

I am Yours truly,

A.M.Elliott
