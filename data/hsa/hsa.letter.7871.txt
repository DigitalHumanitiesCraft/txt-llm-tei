London

My dear Sir, 
Thanks for your letter of the 22nd May, and the several Pamphlets on Creole Languages: As soon as you are able to prepare a paper for the pages of the Journal of the Royal Asiatic Society I will publish it.
My Book on the Languages of Africa (note 1) is now in the Press – I draw attention to the subject of your inquiries – I will always let you know if I hear of anything –
Aristide Marre published „Notice sur la Langue Portugaise dans l’Inde française, et en Malaisie“. (note 2)
In the Annales de l’Extême Orient – Paris I have a copy of it; perhaps you have seen it –
Mr 
Christaller (note 3) has paid me a visit, and gone back to Schorndorf – The Bishops in India and the East are
