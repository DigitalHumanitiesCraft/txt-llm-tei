/4/84
 64, St. George’s Square,
 S. W. 
Dear Sir /
Dr Rost (note 1) has written to me that you expect from me some books published last year by Schön, Stuart and Williams: I have referred to your last letter of Jan 16, and know not what you wish: Please name the books. (note 2)
Archdeacon Johnson (note 3) (the Negro) will be in London in a few days.
Many thanks for the Creolische Studien Part VI (note 4)
I conclude you have seen Whitneys (of New Haven) paper in the America Philological Transactions on „Mixture of Languages“ (note 5) There is a paper on Kurdo Jewish by Löwy in J of Soc Biblical Archaeology 1877 (note 6)
See also Haldeman Pennsylvania Dutch Philadelphia U. S. 1873 (note 7) I have a review of it –
See also „Sailor’s Language“ Clark Russell, Sampson Low, 1883 (note 8)
Also Laskari Dictionary Nautical Terms – Roebuck Allen – 1882 5
th Edition (note 9)
I include a paper (note 10) which gives all the information which I  possess – I have gone over the whole bundle, and I have nothing beyond what I have written – 
I have come across (??)
Secret Languages –
geheimsprache
I Zanzibar page 349 of my Book
Ki-Nyume (note 11)
II Pongwe, page 419 of my Book
Eurizia –
III Johnston – „Kongo“ 1884 (note 12)
mysterious form of Kongo not taught to women
Yours truly
Robert Cust
