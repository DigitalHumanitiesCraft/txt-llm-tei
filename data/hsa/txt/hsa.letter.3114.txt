28 Oct. 00 15 Rawlinson Rd, Oxford
I am much obliged to you for sending me your 
Klassifikation der Rom. Mund. (note 1) I have read it with great interest. It is an important historical document showing how much you were in advance of your contemporaries, & how long ago you had articulated the ‘new views’.
H. Sweet
