London

 28/6/83 S. W. 
Dear Sir/ 
Your letter of June 9: If you require Goldie, Efik Dictionary, (note 1) write to Friederich Muller, (note 2) and bow his copy: he has the book; if this does not succeed – write to Dr R Rost – India Office: Westminster (note 3) – he promises to lend you his copy
Write a letter in English to Archdeacon Dandeson Crowther, (note 4) son of the Bishop, who is now in England – his address is Church Mission Home  Salisbury Square London: he will reply – the dd (note 5) Bishop has no time.
Kohler, book on Bonny Sprache (Gottingen) is not worth having. I have a copy I have small Primers or Vocabularies of 
Idzo } all on
Ibo } the
Igaza } Lower
Igbira } Niger
I could lend them to you if you will wait till my Book is out – it will be published in September, and will tell  you a great deal
Nothing has been published about the Calabar Languages except Goldie’s Books (note 6) – I know nothing about the Neger gehein Sprachen, (note 7) to which you allude, nor have I seen Dr Bartrams (note 8) article on that subject. 
The Bishops of Calcutta and Bombay would not reply to letters on such subjects – they are too busy
Yours faithfully
Robert Cust
 T.O. (note 9)

Of course you have Koelles Polyglotta africána (note 10)
