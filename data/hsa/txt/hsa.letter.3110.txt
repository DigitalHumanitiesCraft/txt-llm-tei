Heath Str. Hampstead. London 22/6/85.
Dear Sir,
Thanks for your kind letter. I am very glad that my Welsh paper (note 1) is likely to be of some use to you. I hope before very long to bring out a phraseological thesaurus of English, as I have now cleared off a great deal of my Old-English work (note 2), and intend to devote myself henceforth as much as possible to living philology (note 3). The new views have really been developed, to a great extent, independantly and simultaneously in Germany and England; perhaps we have a slight priority, but it is hardly worth disputing about.
H. Sweet
