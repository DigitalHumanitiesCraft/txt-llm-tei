 Frere Lodge, Ashburton Road, 
 Croydon, England. 
 May 21, 1883 
Sir, -
 Yours of May 15 just to hand. 
 The Rev. Lorimer Fison, M. A., (note 1) formerly of the Cambridge University, now Intor (note 2) of our College in Fiji for training native agents, is pre-eminently the man who can give all possible information of language in the South Pacific. 
 His wife and family are near Melbourne, for health; and he will be likely to join them at the end of this year. 
I am yours truly

James Calvert

 Dr. Hugo Schuchardt
