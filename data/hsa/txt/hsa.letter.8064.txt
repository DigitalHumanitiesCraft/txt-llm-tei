Imperial Fisheries Bureau
 Tokyo 15 June 1900
Dear Sir,
I have received your note dated 20 April in due time. There is no comprehensive work on the fishing apparatus of our country. You must get informations about them from various sources, chiefly from the books written in Japanese. I am now investigating our fishing apparatus and hope eagerly to hear about the similar works in other countries. I shall publish the result of my investigation in English from time to time. (note 1)
I send you this time my short paper on our fishing industry and a book written in Japanese but with many illustrations.
We have no trammel; (note 2) but there is a partly double-walled net. This net is in use in a particular district only.
